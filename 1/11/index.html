Follow the Fish

2025-01-11

The journey of visually investigating the perceptions of machines continues. I am curious to see if I can improve temporal coherency in the understanding of a video, making the captions less sensitive to subtle changes.

Going back to the video from 1/1 of a fish in an aquarium seemed like a good thing to do because there are no scene transitions and not a whole lot going on. It seems like this video would have a much tighter region of white space, and I found that was indeed the case.

Most samples concentrate in a small region, and the jumps are basically descriptions of rocks instead of fish.

So many frame annotations cluster in a small region that I realize this presents some visualization problems for me, because the detail isn’t visible.
